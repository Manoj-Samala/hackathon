About Quadrant

Quadrant Technologies is a global provider of IT services, products, and solutions across diverse industries. We are a ‘Cloud & Data’ company involved in building and delivering managed services. Since our inception in 2004, we have been meeting the business needs of our customers more effectively while maintaining priorities for cost and security. We specialize in diverse services like Cloud, Data & Analytics, Accessibility, Business Intelligence, Quality Testing, DevOps, AI & ML, CRM, ERP, Big Data, and Application Development.

While meeting our customers’ changing needs in terms of strategy, technique, and organization management, we have a proven record of accomplishments in delivering innovative solutions and achieving the best results that exceed our customers expectations. We put our clients first - by getting to know them, building a strong & lasting partnership, and doing what is important to them.

Our Core Values

Quadrant Technologies is an equal-opportunity employer, and all employment decisions are based on merit and business needs. We believe that finding peace and joy in our professional journey is an invaluable part of sustainable and lasting success. This is put into effect by supplying a work environment free of discrimination and harassment.

We are passionate about achieving the highest standards of quality. Our professionals are very conversant with the latest developments and transform the project work model according to our client’s business strategy and process. Timely updates (weekly/monthly) and clients feedbacks support our team to complete the projects on time with a higher level of customer satisfaction. Our expert team also maintains trust, security, and confidentiality for all our clients. We follow strict non-disclosure policies towards client and project information.
